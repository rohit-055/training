About CCTNS

The Crime and Criminal Tracking Network & Systems (CCTNS) is a project initiated by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, to create a comprehensive and integrated system for effective policing in the country. It aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of police departments across India by providing a robust IT infrastructure.

  • Real-time crime tracking and information sharing
  • Improved data management and analysis capabilities
  • Enhanced coordination among various law enforcement agencies
